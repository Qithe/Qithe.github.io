The 9X21 is for the beginner who is just getting started in gaming or for the veteran who want an small and portable rig. For the ultimate gaming experience, the 9X59 delivers. The hyperpowerd Threadripper central prossesing unit can any load thrown at it, with its dual radiator cooling apparatus.
